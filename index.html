Magellan’s Cross

Historic landmark marking the arrival of Christianity in Cebu City.

10,000 Roses

A beautiful LED rose garden in Cordova — perfect for evening strolls & photos.

Cebu Taoist Temple

A colorful temple built by the Chinese community, offering panoramic views of Cebu City.

Temple of Leah

Roman-inspired temple built as a symbol of love, with grand architecture and statues.

Mactan Island

Tropical paradise with resorts, white sandy beaches, and diving spots.

Cebu Lechon

Famous for crispy skin & flavorful meat — considered the BEST lechon in the Philippines.

Kinilaw

Fresh raw fish marinated in vinegar, ginger, and spices — a classic Filipino appetizer.

Sugba

Grilled seafood or meat, served fresh from local markets — Cebuano street food delight.

Baked Scallops

Scallops baked with butter, garlic, and cheese — popular Cebuano seafood treat.

Danggit

Sun-dried fish, usually fried until crispy — a staple breakfast item in Cebu.

Pintos

Cebuano-style grilled or baked snacks, often made of dough or rice-based ingredients.

Shangri-La Mactan Cebu

Luxury resort with pristine beaches, fine dining, and world-class amenities.

Mövenpick Hotel Mactan Island Cebu

Modern beachfront resort with swimming pools, restaurants, and spectacular sunset views.

Radisson Blu Cebu

Elegant city hotel with easy access to business districts, shopping, and cultural sites.
